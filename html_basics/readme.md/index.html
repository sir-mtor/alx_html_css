WELCOME TO MY ALX SPACE

ONBOARDING

Onboarding is the phase you as a new ALX learner go through in between making the decision that you want to start this course, and being a fully set up learner for this programe, knowing exactly how use the most the tools and resources needed for the programe.

INTRODUCTION

This is the phase explaining what the program is about. It should include key details about the programe that give the leaner enough information to understand the purpose and scope of the course.

PEER GROUPING

Peer grouping is the social phase of the course, allowing learners to be sub divided into smaller groups to share and discuss topics that seem challenging. This is where learners can escape supervision from moderators and learn on their own. The influence of the peer group helps learners to understand more while they teach their peers.
